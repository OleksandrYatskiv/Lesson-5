Best Luxury Sports Cars for 2022 and 2023

2022 Chevrolet Corvette

The 2022 Chevrolet Corvette claims the top spot in the luxury sports car class, thanks to its value proposition, lightning-quick acceleration, precise handling, comfortable and well-trimmed interior, and intuitive tech features.

2022 Porsche Cayman

The 2022 Porsche Cayman is everything you want a luxury sports car to be: fast, agile, and posh. However, some rivals offer more standard features.

2022 Porsche Boxster

The 2022 Porsche Boxster climbs toward the top of the luxury sports car class partly on the strength of its well-rounded performance and high predicted reliability score.

2022 Porsche 911

The 2022 Porsche 911 ranks highly among luxury sports cars thanks in part to its enticing acceleration, standard-setting handling, and impeccable quality.

2023 BMW 8-Series

The 2023 BMW 8 Series combines thrilling performance with a luxurious cabin, representing the best of what the brand has to offer.

2021 Lexus LC

The 2021 Lexus LC finishes in the top half of our luxury sports car rankings. It's fun to drive, with a first-rate interior and a comfortable ride, even if it doesn't have the blinding athleticism of some classmates.

2022 Lexus LC

The 2022 Lexus LC ranks midpack among luxury sports cars. It delivers spry acceleration, well-rounded handling attributes, and a classy cabin, but it comes up short in rear-seat and cargo space, and its infotainment controls are cumbersome.
